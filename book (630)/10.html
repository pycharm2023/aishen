中文标语

神救恩生机的一面

一九九六年二月新春华语特会

神完整的救恩有法理及生机的两面。

法理的一面，是神救恩的手续，借基督的救赎，使神的救恩完全合乎祂公义律法的要求。

生机的一面，是神救恩的成全，借基督的生命，使信徒在神的生命上变化长大而成熟。

神的救恩，是以基督为中心，以基督的身体为路线，并以新耶路撒冷为目标，全由那赐生命的灵来完成。

英文标语

The Organic Aspect of God's Salvation

Chinese New Year's Conference, February 1996

The complete salvation of God has two aspects - the judicial aspect and the organic aspect.

The judicial aspect is the procedure of God's salvation through the redemption of Christ for God's salvation to fully meet the righteous requirement of His law.

The organic aspect is the fulfillment of God's salvation through the life of Christ for the believers to be transformed and to grow and mature in the life of God.

The salvation of God is with Christ as the center, the Body of Christ as the line, and the New Jerusalem as the goal, and is fully accomplished through the life-giving Spirit.
